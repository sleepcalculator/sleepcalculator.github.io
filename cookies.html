Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

This is the Cookies Policy for SleepCalculator, accessible from https://sleepcalculator.github.io/. To optimize performance and transparently log operational user sessions, our site drops tiny local identifiers called cookies onto your device.

1. What Are Cookies

As is common practice with almost all professional web portals, this site uses cookies, which are tiny text files downloaded to your computer, to improve your browsing workflow. This page describes what information they gather, how we use them, and why we sometimes need to store these cookies. We will also share how you can prevent these cookies from being stored, though this may downgrade or break certain elements of the site's structural interface utility.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons detailed below. Unfortunately, in most cases, there are no industry standard options for disabling cookies without completely disabling the functionality and features they add to this site. It is recommended that you leave on all cookies if you are unsure whether you need them or not, in case they are used to provide a service that you use.

3. Disabling Cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the configuration options within your browser settings menu (consult your specific browser's Help resource to learn how). Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functional behavior of this and many other sites that you visit. Disabling cookies will usually result in also disabling certain features of this application. Therefore, it is highly recommended that you do not turn off standard cookies.

4. The Cookies We Set

  • Application Configuration Cookies: In order to provide you with a tailored experience on this site, we provide the functionality to set your preferences for how this tool runs when you use it (e.g., preference configurations for Age Groups or Target Sleep Durations). To remember your preferences, we need to set cookies so that this information can be recalled whenever you interact with a page influenced by your configuration settings.

5. Third Party Analytics Tracking

In special cases, we also utilize tracking assets provided by verified third parties. The following section details which third-party cookies you might encounter through using SleepCalculator.

  • This site uses Google Analytics, which is one of the most widespread and trusted web metrics solutions, to help us understand how you navigate the platform and discover methods to refine your user experience. These scripts may track variables such as your time spent browsing or specific pages visited so we can continue to create high-quality, high-ranking educational copy.
